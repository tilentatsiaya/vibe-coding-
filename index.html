Malaria Prevention Strategies

Use Bed Nets

Sleep under insecticide-treated mosquito nets (ITNs) to prevent mosquito bites.

Indoor Spraying

Use indoor residual spraying (IRS) with insecticides to control mosquito populations.

Preventive Medication

Take antimalarial drugs as preventive treatment if you're in high-risk areas.

Protective Clothing

Wear long-sleeved clothing and pants, especially during evening hours.
